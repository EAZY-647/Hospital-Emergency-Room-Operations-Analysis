ğŸ“ˆ Analysis & Insights

Demographics: The analysis reveals a balanced gender distribution and identifies Adults (18-65) as the highest volume age group.

Operational Challenges: While the 35-minute average wait time is stable, the low satisfaction score (4.99) indicates a need for better patient communication.

Resource Misuse: A high volume of patients (over 1,800) are being referred to General Practice, suggesting the ER is frequently used for non-emergency issues.
